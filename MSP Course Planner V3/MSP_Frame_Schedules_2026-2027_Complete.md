## Notes on Schedule Organization

1. **Column System**: Courses are organized in columns A-G corresponding to specific time blocks throughout the week
2. **Practical Selection Rule**: Students cannot select a practical in the same column as their chosen courses
3. **Available Columns**: The rightmost columns in the practical tables indicate which columns (and therefore which course timeslots) the practical does NOT conflict with
4. **Co-requisites (◊)**: Must be taken simultaneously with another specified course
5. **Prerequisites (▲)**: Requires completion of specified prior courses
6. **Special Locations**: 
   - ** = Campus Venlo courses
   - * = Other special locations or conditions (see individual course notes)
